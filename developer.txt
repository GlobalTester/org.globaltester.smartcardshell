The libraries provided within SmartCardShell are either included in this plugin or moved to org.globaltester.core.
This is required because the cryptographic functionality may be needed in use cases where the SmartCardShell itself is dispensable.

Currently SmartCardShelll version 3.7.989 is used.

Jars included in org.globaltester.smartcardshell:
ocf
scdp4j
scsh3


Jars included in org.globaltester.core:
bcmail
bcprov
js
xmldsig
